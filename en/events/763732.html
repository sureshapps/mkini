Top Tech Innovation Awards 2026: Spotlighting the architects of Malaysia’s digital future

For three years, we’ve spotlighted the visionaries rewriting Malaysia’s digital DNA. In 2026, the stakes are higher, the stage is bigger, and the competition is fiercer.

Endorsed by MOSTI and organised by Malaysiakini and Digital News Asia, the Top Tech Innovation Awards 2026 are seeking the breakthroughs defining our era – from AI and Automation to Space Tech and Sustainability. Whether you’re a young innovator breaking boundaries or a global giant setting new standards, this is your platform.

The road to tech excellence is a marathon, not a sprint. The journey culminates on Nov 26, 2026 with a celebration of the minds that dared to build what’s next.

Your countdown to the stage begins now.
